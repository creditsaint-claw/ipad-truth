👁️ Section 5

Your eyes are
changing right now.

Ages 8–13 are the peak window for myopia (nearsightedness) development. A 2024 study of 19 countries found heavy screen users have 2.3× higher risk. Once your eyes change shape, it doesn't reverse.

✅ Normal Eye

Light focuses exactly on the retina. Clear vision near and far. Eye stays round and healthy.

⚠️ Myopic Eye (Screen User)

Eye grows too long. Light focuses in front of the retina — blurry distance vision. The eye shape change is permanent.

The fix is simple and proven: 1–2 hours outside every day. Natural light regulates eye growth. This is one of the most researched, most effective interventions available — and it costs nothing.
